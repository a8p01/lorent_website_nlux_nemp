Our Story Since 1975

Founded in Switzerland by master watchmaker Henri Lorent, our company began with a simple mission: create quality timepieces that everyone could afford. What started as a small workshop has grown into a global brand trusted by watch enthusiasts worldwide.

We've never forgotten our roots. Every Lorent watch is still designed with the same attention to detail and commitment to value that Henri established nearly 50 years ago. We believe luxury shouldn't come with a luxury price tag.

Today, we continue to innovate while staying true to our core values: quality, affordability, and exceptional customer service. When you choose Lorent, you're joining a legacy of craftsmanship that spans generations.
