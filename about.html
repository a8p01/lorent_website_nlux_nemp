About Lorent

For nearly 50 years, we've been on a mission to make quality timepieces accessible to everyone. From our humble beginnings in a Swiss workshop to serving customers worldwide, our commitment remains the same: exceptional watches at prices that make sense.

Our Founding Story

In 1975, master watchmaker Henri Lorent opened a small workshop in the Swiss Alps with a revolutionary idea: why should quality timepieces be reserved for the wealthy? Working with a team of skilled craftsmen, Henri developed techniques to create reliable, beautiful watches without the luxury markup.

What started as a local business quickly grew as word spread about our exceptional value. Henri's philosophy was simple: "Every person deserves a watch they can be proud of, regardless of their budget."

Today, that same spirit drives everything we do. We've never forgotten our roots or our mission to democratize quality watchmaking.

Quality Without Compromise

We believe that affordable doesn't mean cheap. Every Lorent watch undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our strict quality standards. From the precision of the movement to the durability of the materials, we never cut corners where it matters.

Our direct-to-consumer approach eliminates unnecessary markups from middlemen and fancy boutiques. Instead, we invest in what truly matters: superior components, precise assembly, and comprehensive testing.

The result? Watches that rival those costing 3-4 times more, available at prices that real people can afford.
